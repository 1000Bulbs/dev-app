Hello, and welcome to the fizzbuzz app.

The goal of this challenge is to write a program that prints the numbers from 1 to 100.

The following acceptance criteria is needed:

  • For multiples of 3 print "Fizz" instead of the number.
  • For multiples of 5 print "Buzz" instead of the number.
  • For multiples of both 3 and 5 print "FizzBuzz" instead of the number.
  • For numbers not a multiple of 3, 5, or both just print the number.
